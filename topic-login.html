How to Log In

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Open the CareChamp app on your device.
  2. Enter your username (usually your email address).
  3. Type your password carefully.
  4. Tap the Log In button.
  5. If you forgot your password, click Forgot Password? to reset it.
