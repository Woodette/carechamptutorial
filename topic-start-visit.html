Start a Visit

How to Begin Your Scheduled Visit

  1. From the Home screen, find your scheduled visit for the day.
  2. Tap on the visit to open the visit details.
  3. Tap the Start Visit button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. You may be prompted to allow location access — this is required for EVV (Electronic Visit Verification).
  5. Once the visit is started, the timer will begin, and documentation options will appear.

If the Start Visit button is grayed out or missing, make sure you are within the scheduled time window and have internet access.
